06 — How we ship in 14 days

Four phases. Two weeks. One launch.

  1. 01

    Discovery Days 1 – 2

    Brief, audit, and a 90-minute strategy call. We map your business goal, your funnel and the single outcome the new site has to deliver — before a single pixel is moved.

  2. 02

    Design Days 3 – 7

    High-fidelity design in Figma — every section, every interaction, every state. One revision round built into the timeline so we ship a design you'd happily put in a brand book.

  3. 03

    Build Days 8 – 12

    Hand-coded in Next.js or as a static site. Cinematic motion, sub-1-second load, mobile-perfect. CMS wiring, analytics and SEO foundations included — not bolted on later.

  4. 04

    Launch Days 13 – 14

    QA, cross-device test, deploy to Vercel or Cloudflare, hand over the keys. Thirty days of post-launch refinements are included — you ship with us, not at us.
